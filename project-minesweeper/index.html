How to Play

Mines are hidden in the grid of squares! Click any square to start.

Safe squares have numbers telling you how many mines touch the square. If you click on a mine you lose the game!

Right click to flag a square without opening it. The counter shows the number of mines without flags, and a clock shows your time in seconds.

To reset the game, click on the emoji. To choose difficulty, go to Game on the menu bar.

The game ends when all safe squares have been opened.
